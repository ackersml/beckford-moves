# Wix AI Design Prompt for Beckford Fitness

## Business Overview
Create a modern, professional website for **Beckford Fitness** - a personalized 1-on-1 fitness coaching, life coaching, and nutrition guidance business led by Sean Beckford, an 11-year experienced trainer with 9 professional certifications.

## Design Inspiration & Reference Sites
Study and incorporate design elements from these three reference websites:
1. **Massage Strong** (massagestrong.com) - Clean service-focused layout, clear CTAs, professional communication emphasis
2. **Functional Bodybuilding** (functional-bodybuilding.com) - Bold typography, modern minimalist aesthetic, strong hero sections, testimonial-focused design
3. **Living Waters Therapies** (livingwaterstherapies.ca) - Calm, soothing wellness aesthetic, organized service cards, holistic approach presentation

## Design Aesthetic
- **Style:** Modern, clean, professional wellness/fitness aesthetic
- **Tone:** Approachable yet authoritative, inspiring transformation
- **Color Palette:** Professional fitness/wellness colors (consider deep blues, greens, or neutral tones with accent colors)
- **Typography:** Bold, clear headings with readable body text (similar to Functional Bodybuilding's strong typography)
- **Layout:** Clean, organized sections with clear visual hierarchy
