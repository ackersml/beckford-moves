## ✏️ How to Edit Your Website Content

Your website has a built-in content management system (CMS) that lets you edit content without touching any code!

### Accessing the Admin Dashboard

1. Go to: **https://beckford-site.vercel.app/studio**
2. Log in with your Sanity account (I'll help you set this up)
3. You'll see a dashboard where you can manage:

| Section | What You Can Edit |
|---------|------------------|
| **Testimonials** | Client reviews, names, photos |
| **Blog Posts** | Write new articles, add images |
| **Packages** | Pricing, features, descriptions |
| **Services** | Service descriptions and features |
| **Site Settings** | Contact email, social links, etc. |

### Quick Editing Tips

- **To add a new testimonial:**
  1. Click "Testimonials" in the sidebar
  2. Click "+ Create" 
  3. Fill in the client name, their quote, and rating
  4. Click "Publish"

- **To write a blog post:**
  1. Click "Blog Posts" in the sidebar
  2. Click "+ Create"
  3. Add your title, choose a category, write your content
  4. Add an image if you want
  5. Click "Publish"

- **Changes appear on your site within seconds!**
